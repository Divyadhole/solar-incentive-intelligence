US Solar Incentive Intelligence

Which states make the most financial sense for commercial solar right now? This dashboard combines EIA 2023 electricity rates, NREL solar resource data, and DSIRE state incentive programs to score every US state. Arizona ranks #5 nationally — 13.0% IRR on a 100kW commercial system. High-rate states like Hawaii (38.8¢/kWh) and California (23.1¢/kWh) lead on returns. States with strong incentive programs average 2.1 years faster payback.
